The brief

Change comms that feel human.

Internal decks are usually forgettable. This one had to make a re-org feel both exciting and reassuring — to carry a whole company through a new operating model in one all-hands.

The system is a fully dark, aurora-lit editorial: big confident grotesque headlines with a Playfair-italic accent on the key word, translucent panels, pill elements and soft cobalt glows. Premium and modern — never corporate.
