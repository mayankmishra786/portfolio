The brief

Change comms that feel human.

Internal decks are usually forgettable. This one had to make a re-org feel exciting and safe at the same time, in one all-hands.

Fully dark, with blue glows. Big headlines, one word set in italic serif, glass panels and pills. Premium, not corporate.

Approach

Glow with discipline.

01

Italic accent

One word per headline turns to a Playfair italic — "work", "One Vantage", "Questions?".

02

Glow moments

Soft cobalt-violet auroras light only the cover, the vision and the close — never the data.

03

Translucent system

Fine-bordered glass panels and pill tags keep the dark UI calm and legible.
