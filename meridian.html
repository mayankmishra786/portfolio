Meridian Financial Group

A quarterly earnings deck for a bank. Black and white, one red accent, built so analysts can skim it.

Client
Meridian Financial (illustrative)
Sector
Banking & finance
Deck
Investor · 10 slides
Role
Art direction · copy · build
The brief

Earnings, made legible.

Investor decks drown in numbers. The job was to show a full quarter, revenue through buybacks, without it reading like a spreadsheet.

Analysts skim. So one idea per slide, the key number set huge, and one red accent to point the eye.

Design system

Monochrome Swiss, one hot accent.

Approach

Built to be skimmed.

01

Light / dark alternation

Slides flip between paper and ink to pace the narrative and separate sections.

02

Oversized data

The single most important figure on each slide is the largest thing on it.

03

Disciplined accent

Vermillion appears once per slide — a rule, a delta, or one number — never as filler.
