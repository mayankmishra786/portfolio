MHV

I believe a great deck is engineered — equal parts editor, designer and strategist.

I design presentations for enterprises across finance, pharma, industrials, consumer goods and software — work that has to be premium, precise, and on-brand under pressure.

My background blends visual design with a love of structure: I obsess over hierarchy, restraint and craft, because that’s what separates a deck that informs from one that persuades.

Every project here was art-directed, written and built end-to-end — distinct systems proving range, not a single look on repeat.
