The brief

Industrial, but expensive.

Industrial software decks usually go dark with neon glow. I wanted the opposite: something that feels like a precision-engineering brand, not a gamer UI.

Raw concrete, one rust accent, big condensed type against a serif. The numbers carry the ROI. This replaced an earlier neon version. Restraint won.
