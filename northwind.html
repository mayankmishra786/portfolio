The brief

A house of brands, considered.

It had to feel like a fashion house’s annual report. Ivory and bordeaux, a high-contrast serif, wide margins. Quiet, not glossy.

Restraint is the point. Hairline number grids, a dotted-map footprint, and one full-bordeaux quote slide to let it breathe.
